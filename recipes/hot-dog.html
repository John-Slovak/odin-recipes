Hot Dog

Description

Delicious Hot-Dog with BBQ sauce and some chopped onions.
Delicatesy especially with some beer or wine.

Ingredients

  • 3 Sausages
  • 1/4 Onion
  • 3 Breads
  • BBQ Sauce

Steps

  1. Let abou 2L of water cook in a cooking pot untill it boils.
  2. Spread 3 sausages on a chopping board, and slice the surface slightly on both sides in the shapes of little X's.
  3. Chuck the sausages inside the boiling water and cover it with a lid. Let it cook for about 7-8 minutes.
  4. Chop up about 1/4th of an onion in the meantime, slice up the breads to fit the sausages, and squirt litle bit of BBQ sauce inside of them.
  5. Take out the sausages, and fit them inside the breads.
  6. Squirt some more BBQ sauce on top in a squiggly pattern, and add the chopped onions.
  7. Serve. Able to eat imidiately.
