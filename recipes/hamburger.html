Nachos Hamburger

Description

A juicy hamburger filled with fried patty, cheese, BBQ sauce,
salad, onions, and cheetos. Takes a little effort to make, but
the result is definitely more than worth it.

Ingredients

  • 2 Buns
  • BBQ Sauce
  • 1/4 Onion
  • Green Salad
  • 2 Cheese Slices
  • Pre-made Meat
  • Nachos

Steps

  1. Form 2 patties from the pre-made meat, and put them on a pan with olive oil to fry. Switch sides every 2/3 minutes until done.
  2. Slice up the 2 buns in half, and put them on a clean pan to fry the insides just a little bit. Should take no more than 2 minutes.
  3. Prepare the two bottom buns on a plate, and squirt some BBQ sauce on both.
  4. Cut up the onion, and sprinkle it on top of the BBQ sauce.
  5. Layer couple of layers of salad on top, followed by couple squirts of BBQ sauce, and 3-4 nachos.
  6. When the patties are done, put them on top of the nachos.
  7. Lay the two cheese slices on top of the patties.
  8. squirt some BBQ sauce on the cheese, followed by 3-4 nachos, couple more layers of salad, a bit more BBQ sauce, and finally the top bun.
  9. Squeeze both burgers lightly, untill you hear the nachos crack.
  10. Ready to serve. Able to eat imidiately.
